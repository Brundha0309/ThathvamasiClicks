Who We Are

Passionate Storytellers with a Camera

Welcome to Thathvamasi Clicks — a boutique photography studio dedicated to capturing life's most meaningful moments with artistry and heart. Our name, rooted in Tamil culture, means "That which you are" — reflecting our belief that authentic moments define us.

Founded with a deep love for visual storytelling, we specialize in weddings, family portraits, corporate events, and everything in between. With over 5 years of experience and 500+ happy clients, we bring technical excellence and genuine warmth to every shoot.

We don't just take photographs — we create timeless memories that you and your family will cherish for generations.
